----- MsStore -----

Name: 
Piq - Color Picker

Current published version: "<AppVersion>1.0.2</AppVersion>"
1.0.2

Desc:
Lightweight and intuitive color picker for Windows. Capture any screen pixel, fine-tune the selection with mouse or keyboard post-capture, and copy colors in HEX, RGB, HSV, HSL, and CMYK formats.

Features:
Capture colors from any pixel on your screen.
Select the exact pixel with mouse or keyboard post-capture.
Copy colors in HEX, RGB, HSV, HSL, and CMYK formats.
Global hotkey to start and stop capture.
Always-on-top window option.
Multi-monitor support.
Persistent user settings.
Lightweight, intuitive, and easy to use.

Privacy policy:
Piq does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All screen capture and color processing is performed locally on your device and is never uploaded or sent to any third party.
