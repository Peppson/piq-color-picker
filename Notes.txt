Desc long:
Lightweight and intuitive color picker for Windows. Capture any screen pixel, fine-tune the selection with mouse or keyboard after capture, and copy colors in HEX, RGB, HSV, HSL, and CMYK formats.

Desc Short:
Lightweight and intuitive color picker for Windows. Capture any screen pixel, fine-tune the selection with mouse or keyboard after capture, and copy colors in various formats.

Features:
Capture colors from any pixel on your screen.
Select the exact pixel with mouse or keyboard post-capture.
Copy colors in HEX, RGB, HSV, HSL, and CMYK formats.
Global hotkey to start and stop capture.
Always-on-top window option.
Multi-monitor support.

Privacy policy:
PiQ does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All screen capture and color processing is performed locally on your device. No images, colors, or other data are uploaded or shared with any third party.

Notes:
PiQ is a classic desktop color picker that requires desktop interop.
Specific APIs/features used: Global hotkey registration, screen capture across apps and the desktop, popup message window.

> check version num 
> select x86 and x64 archiectures - Release
